Classic Lemon

  • Lemons are used for both culinary and non-culinary purposes throughout the world. It is most commonly known for being used in juices!
  • Source of: Vitamin C, Vitamin A, and Vitamin B5

Acai
Berry

Acai Berry

  • Acai berries are small, round, and black-purple. They resemble grapes!
  • Source of: Amino Acids (which help promote muscle performance), energy, endurance and strength.

Passion
Fruit

Passion Fruit

  • Passion fruits have a juicy edible center composed of a large number of seeds. They can be red, yellow, purple, or green in color.
  • Source of: Vitamin A and Vitamin C
